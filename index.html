Welcome to LeftHanded Studios

Lefties for Life

Our Mission

Are you left handed? Have you ever struggled to use everyday objects that were made with a bias for right handers? If so, this is the place for you! LeftHanded Studios aims to create new products and goods tailored specifically to the needs of left handed customers.

At LeftHanded Studios, we believe in creating innovative industrial design solutions that challenge conventions and push boundaries. Our passion lies in crafting products that are not only functional but also beautiful, sustainable, and user-centered.

Our goal is to empower lefties to thrive and succeed in a world of right handed rulers with tools that they can now use just as easily as everyone else.

We are dedicated to bringing fresh perspectives to the world of industrial design, with a focus on creativity, quality, and attention to detail. Whether it's reimagining everyday objects or developing cutting-edge concepts, we approach every project with enthusiasm and expertise.

We appreciate any feedback or sugegtions you may have. We'd love to hear your great ideas because after all, lefties are always right!

Join us on our journey as we continue to grow, innovate, and create designs that make a difference.

Meet the Creators

Get to know the talented individuals behind LeftHanded Studios.

Nicolas Nagel

Role: Co-Founder

Bio: Nico Nagel grew up as a natural leftie. After being taught in preschool that he had to "only ever write your name with your right hand" he was quickly converted to a right-hander. But not being a leftie just didn't feel right for him. He has always felt like some part of him was missing from him ever since that fateful day. Now, he mostly still uses his right hand, but ocassionally gravitates towards ambidexterity. In his free time, he enjoys reading, knitting, crocheting, learning about hypnotism, running, and making other fun crafts!

Email: ninagel0709@markubanai.org

Eva Nagel

Role: Co-Founder

Bio: Eva Nagel grew up in Durham, North Carolina with her triplet brother, Nico. In her free time, she enjoys reading, crafting, exercising, and relaxing. She has always naturally gravitated towards the field of industrial design. In fact, this passion was one of the main factors leading to the creation of LeftHanded Studios. Her passion for creativity and her determination to achieve her ambitions empowers her to give her very best to all the proucts she designs.

Email: evnagel0709@markcubanai.org

Mark Cuban AI

Role: Inspiration and Place of Origin

Bio: Mark Cuban AI created a bootcamp for high school kids. One year, Nico and Eva Nagel decided to apply to this bootcamp, and they got in! They learned all about the proper ways to use AI, and how it worked. Finally, on the third and final week of the bootcamp, they created LeftHanded Studios with the use of AI as a capstone project to show what they learned. Mark Cuban AI was there for LeftHanded Studios since the beginning, and has been ever since.

Email: hi@markcubanai.org
